Spesific Goals

User Goal Content/Action
Sponsors Attract sponsors to supply the program with sports equipment, facilities and resources for sports, academics and facilities.. Make use of the website and existing connections with friends and references.
Donors/Financial Partners Attract individuals, businesses and companies to donate funds to the organization. As a registered NPO we provide a transparent channel for the business community to invest their "BEE" contributions through structured enterprise development and social upliftment programs
Volunteers Reaching athletes, tutors and mentors to volunteer their time and expertise to spend time teaching and encouraging the struggling youth. Using registered funding offices in the USA (Georgia, Atlanta) and the UK (Oxford) where all the administration and management of our program projects will take place.
Adults and Parents Provide Communities with the support and tools needed to provide a balanced meal to the poor and under-privileged by means of donations (Maize, oil, etc) but also through education in self-sustaining activities such as farming. Adults are equipped with the necessary tools for creating communal vegetable gardens to grow their own food in their back gardens providing them with a sustainable source of food for their families.
Struggling Youth Offer hope, a future, and a safe space to play, study and live comfortably. Moreover, to provide mentorship and Nutrition. ENGAGE (through activities) - ESTABLISH (values/relationships) - EQUIP (tools to succeed) - EMPOWER (creating opportunities for life)
Local Organizations in Ficksburg Help startup and support emerging charities and local programs so that they can continue to help the local community on their own. Using some of the allocated funds as well as volunteering from Active Investors.
